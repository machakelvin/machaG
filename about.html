Location

The seminary is located with the judiciary boundary of the catholic diocese of moshi which is in the north eastern of Tanzania at the slopes of mountain Kilimanjaro about 50km from moshi town. The altitude at the seminary is 1666km above the sea level

Historical background

The school was established in 1970 by capuchin missionaries from Switzerland, and started with the small group of 30 students. Since it has grown substantially. Now we have registered students of form I-IV from different parts of Tanzania. The first form IV graduated in 1973. The school was issuing certificate for ordinary level(CSEE) until 1981 when advance level of form V-VI(ACEE) was added.

Why Choose us

With a warm and welcoming learning environment. We place special emphasis on being inclusive and supportive of the diverse needs of a varied and dynamic school community and on offering all students opportunities for growth and success.

Levels of education

The seminary teaches the following subjects in ordinary level: Basic mathematics, Civics, Kiswahili, History, Geography, Chemistry, Physics, Biology and Bible knowledge. Coming to advance level we have different combinations, which are HEG, HGL, EGM. But in 2016 more combinations were added, which are PCM, PCB, CBG and PGM.

Clubs

In order to emphasize in practical and relevant education on Thursdays seminarians go for clubs just after lunch. Example of clubs include fishing club, drawing club and carpentry club. Clubs is amongst the things which make students at Maua seminary to be accurate at their studies due to the relevance of their studies they experience during clubs time

Our mission

To provide a high quality secondary education to the students, with holistic formation aimed at Tanzanian boys for pristely and religious vocation.

Vision

To form responsible persons, committed future leaders of integrity, and to create a society-inspired with divine teachings and instilled wih authentic values, hence acquire the true form of human persons

Specific objective

To have well educated(skilled) and well formed (spiritually and morally upright) students who thrive to become agents for mission of evangelization.
